About Us


Parle Agro, a pioneer in the Indian beverage industry, has been creating innovative products and iconic brands since 1985.

Headquartered in Mumbai, with over 5500+ employees, Parle Agro is the largest Indian beverage company.

Our strength lies in establishing new categories, building brands and capturing market share within those categories. Our bevy of brands include Frooti, Appy, Appy Fizz, BFizz, SMOODH, Bailley, Bailley Soda, Bombay99.

We have always been passionate about building our own brands. We like to create, and we like to nurture what we create. Fueled by innovation that is engrained in the business DNA, our focus and vision is making Parle Agro, the No.1 beverage company in India.

BRANDS

Parle Agro, renowned for its innovative and refreshing beverage offerings, presents an array of brands under its banner, including the widely cherished "Smoodh."

Among its flagship products is "Frooti," a quintessential mango drink celebrated for its distinctive flavor profile and convenient packaging options. Complementing Frooti is "Appy Fizz," an effervescent apple beverage that tantalizes taste buds with its unique fizzy twist. Additionally, Parle Agro offers a diverse range of fruit-based drinks like "Appy" and "Grappo Fizz," each delivering a burst of fruity goodness in every sip.

With its commitment to quality and taste, Parle Agro continues to delight consumers worldwide with its refreshing and rejuvenating beverage portfolio.
